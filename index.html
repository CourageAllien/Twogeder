Why partnerships

Referrals vs paid leads

Referral partnerships

  • ✓ Warm. The customer arrives already trusting you.
  • ✓ No cost per lead, the partnership compounds.
  • ✓ One partner can send clients for years.
  • ✓ Trust transfers from a source the client already uses.

Paid leads

  • – Cold. You earn the trust from scratch every time.
  • – Pay again for every single lead.
  • – Spend stops, leads stop.
  • – Rising costs as more bidders crowd the auction.
